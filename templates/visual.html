Visual Data
Please enter to county you would like to see data on. You can choose between sales data and county data. You can also click back on the Visual Data side navigation to switch between sales or charger data. Please note some counties may have sparse or no data due to their remote locations. This feature it meant to help visualize the increase in charger stations in counties over time and to see the sales of specific vehicle's make. These features may help gauge which counties seem to be expanding their charger station locations and which counties sell more of a certain car brand to see which kind of chargers may be needed and how many.
