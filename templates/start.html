Welcome to a new app that includes a multitude of features for EV drivers on their longer journeys and companies looking to expand their real estate in charger stations.
Our features for EV drivers helps find the nearest charger stations on their long journeys. You will be routed from your starting destination to your ending destination and in-between your total milage will be taken into account to route you to however many charger stations it takes to complete your journey. For example, you are making a 300 mile trip but your EV only has a total range of 80 miles, our app will route you to the nearest stations within your range at least 4 times. You as driver will not need to worry about planning your next charger stop as our app will do it for you, finding the shortest paths along your journey!
Our features for companies helps find new and optimal locations where new charger stations may need to be build. This allows companies to easily plan out where they may find more revenue and customers. Our app takes into account the EV sales in a county and pre-existing locations to predict what counties may need more chargers. As EV sales increase, counties with higher sales may need more charger stations and taking into account the existing charger stations allows us to find locations that seem suited for new charger stations. We also offer a feature for companies to see either the sales data or charger data by county at a quick glance.
